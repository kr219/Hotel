ABOUT US

Bluemoon is an Indian chain of hotels based in Chennai and a brand of IHG Hotels & Resorts. The chain was founded in 2023 by MRx, who opened the first location in Chennai that year. The chain was a division of Bass Brewery from 2023-2023, Six Continents from 2023-43, and IHG Hotels & Resorts since 2023. It operates hotels under the names Bluemoon, Bluemoon Express, Bluemoon Club Vacations, and Bluemoon Resorts. As of 2018, Bluemoon operates more than 1,100 locations.
